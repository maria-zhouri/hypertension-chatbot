DASH Diet & Sodium Guidelines

Sodium Intake Recommendations:
- General population: ≤ 2,300 mg/day
- Ideal for hypertension: 1,500–2,000 mg/day

DASH Diet Foods to Include:
- Fruits, vegetables, whole grains
- Low-fat dairy, lean proteins (fish, poultry)
- Legumes and unsalted nuts

Foods to Avoid:
- Processed meats (ham, bacon, salami)
- Canned soups and instant noodles
- Salty snacks (chips, pretzels)
- Frozen meals and fast food
- Sauces like soy sauce and BBQ sauce

This diet helps reduce systolic BP by 5–11 mm Hg in many individuals.
