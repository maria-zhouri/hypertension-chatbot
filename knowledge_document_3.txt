Lifestyle & Behavior Recommendations for Hypertension

- Physical activity: At least 150 minutes/week of moderate aerobic exercise (e.g. brisk walking)
- Weight management: Aim for healthy BMI (<25)
- Alcohol limits: ≤ 2 drinks/day (men), ≤ 1 drink/day (women)
- Tobacco: Avoid completely
- Stress management: Encourage meditation, yoga, relaxation techniques

Combined lifestyle changes can match or exceed the effectiveness of single medications for hypertension control.

Reminder: This is not medical advice. Always consult a healthcare provider.
