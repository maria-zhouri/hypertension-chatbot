

Definition of Hypertension:
- Normal: ≤ 120/80 mm Hg
- Elevated: 121–134/80–84 mm Hg
- High Risk: ≥ 135/85 mm Hg
- Hypertension: ≥ 140/90 mm Hg (or ≥ 130/80 mm Hg for high-risk individuals or those with diabetes)

Treatment:
- Initiate treatment at ≥ 140/90 mm Hg
- For high-risk patients: begin at 130–139/80–89 mm Hg
- Target: < 130 mm Hg systolic if tolerated

Note: Chatbot should never provide diagnoses. Refer to these numbers only for general awareness.
